Salimini Rume - New York University Abu Dhabi (NYUAD)

Meet our first scholar who achieved their dream of attending Harvard University. Through dedication, hard work, and the support of RGS mentorship program, they overcame financial barriers and academic challenges to secure admission to one of the world's most prestigious institutions. Their journey from a small town in Tanzania to the halls of Harvard is a testament to the power of education and determination.

Our second scholar's acceptance to Stanford University represents years of preparation and commitment. With a passion for technology and innovation, they participated in our SAT preparation program and received comprehensive college application guidance. Their success story demonstrates how proper mentorship and resources can transform dreams into reality, opening doors to world-class education opportunities.
