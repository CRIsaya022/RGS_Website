Salimini Rume - New York University Abu Dhabi (NYUAD)

Hometown: Ghata, Singida

From humble beginnings to earning a full scholarship to NYU Abu Dhabi, Salimini’s journey is a reflection of his unwavering determination and resilience. Despite countless challenges, he never stopped believing in himself and his dreams. His hard work and dedication shine through, and it’s no surprise that his personal statement spoke volumes. Congratulations to you Salimini! Your journey is just beginning, and we can’t wait to see all the amazing things you’ll achieve.

Derick Mushi - Tufts University

Hometown: Moshi, Kilimanjaro

Derick’s successful journey to Tufts University on a full scholarship is a powerful testament to what hard work, consistency, and vision can achieve! From his outstanding academic, personal and social excellence, Derick has been a great inspiration at Rafiki (RGS) and his immediate community. Tufts is incredibly fortunate to have him. His story, his spirit, and everything he’s overcome all speaks for itself. Congratulations, Derick! Your journey is truly inspiring, and your future is full of promise.
