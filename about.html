About Rafiki Global Scholars

Building bridges to higher education in Tanzania

Our Mission

To empower talented Tanzanian students by providing comprehensive support, mentorship, and resources needed to access and succeed in higher education opportunities.

Our Vision

A Tanzania where every deserving student has the opportunity to pursue higher education and become a leader in their community, regardless of their economic background.

Our Story

Founded in 2023, Rafiki Global Scholars emerged from a deep commitment to educational equity in Tanzania. We recognized that many brilliant students lacked the guidance and resources to navigate the complex college application process, and we set out to change that.

Our founders, having witnessed firsthand the untapped potential of Tanzanian youth, established RGS to bridge the gap between talent and opportunity. We believe that education is the most powerful tool for transformation, not just for individuals, but for entire communities.

Our Programs

Comprehensive support for academic success

College Preparation

Comprehensive SAT prep, essay writing workshops, and academic skill development to ensure our scholars are ready for university-level work.

Mentorship

One-on-one guidance from college graduates and professionals who provide ongoing support throughout the application process and beyond.

Scholarships

Financial support for application fees, testing, and educational expenses, removing economic barriers to higher education access.

Rafiki Global Scholars

Empowering Tanzanian students to achieve their dreams through education and creating pathways to global opportunities.
