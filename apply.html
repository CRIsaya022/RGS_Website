How to Apply

Join our community of ambitious scholars

Eligibility Requirements

  • Tanzanian citizen currently in Form 5 or 6
  • Strong academic performance (minimum B+ average)
  • Demonstrated leadership potential
  • Financial need for college access support
  • Commitment to giving back to Tanzania

Application Process

1

Submit Application

Complete our online application form with your academic records, essays, and recommendations.

2

Interview

Selected candidates will be invited for a virtual or in-person interview with our team.

3

Selection

Final selections are made based on academic merit, leadership potential, and program fit.

4

Welcome

Accepted scholars join our program and begin their journey to higher education.
